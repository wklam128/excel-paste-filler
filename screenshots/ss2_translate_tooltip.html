Global Supply Chain Management
Last updated: April 2026  ·  12 min read

A supply chain is a network of organizations, people, activities, information, and resources involved in delivering a product or service to the end consumer. Effective supply chain management reduces costs and improves customer satisfaction.

Supply chain disruptions can significantly impact global trade, leading to shortages and increased prices worldwide.

Modern supply chains rely on real-time data analytics and automation to optimize inventory levels, forecast demand, and coordinate logistics across multiple countries and time zones.
