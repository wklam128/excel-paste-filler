Global Supply Chain Management
Last updated: April 2026  ·  12 min read

A supply chain is a network of organizations, people, activities, information, and resources involved in delivering a product or service to the end consumer.

Supply chain disruptions can significantly impact global trade, leading to shortages and increased prices worldwide.

Modern supply chains rely on real-time data analytics and automation to optimize inventory levels and coordinate logistics across multiple countries.
